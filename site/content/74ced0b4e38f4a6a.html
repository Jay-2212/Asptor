    Uttar Pradesh | Couple, daughter killed as roof collapses during rain in Kasganj

    Three members of a family — a couple and their four-year-old daughter — were killed after the roof of their house collapsed amid heavy rain and storm in Kasganj, officials said today.

    According to the district administration, the incident occurred yesterday in Sheikhupura village under the jurisdiction of the Sidhpura area, while the family members were sleeping inside the house, trapping all occupants under the debris.

    Officials said police and administrative teams pulled out the bodies from under the debris and sent them for post-mortem examination.

    District Magistrate Pranay Singh said the injured children were undergoing treatment and their condition is stable. He said the administration arranged for their treatment, food and other essential requirements.
