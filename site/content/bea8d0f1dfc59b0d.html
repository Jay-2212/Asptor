Delhi HC declines interim relief on plea by Wangchuk's wife seeking transfer from Safdarjung Hospital

Delhi HC declined interim relief on the plea by Sonam Wangchuk’s wife seeking his transfer from Safdarjung Hospital to a hospital of his choice. 

The Court noted that Mr. Wangchuk has been on a hunger strike for 17–18 days and that his medical condition is “perilous.” 

It accepted the Centre’s stand that shifting him to Safdarjung was a medical decision, not an arbitrary one. The Court recorded that his potassium levels are dangerously low, medicines have been administered with his consent, IV fluids have not been given as he has not consented.
