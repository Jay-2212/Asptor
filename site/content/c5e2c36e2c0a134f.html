Delhi High Court agrees to hear on July 24, PIL seeking probe into CJP protest

The Delhi High Court has agreed to hear on Friday (July 23, 2026) a public interest litigation seeking a probe into the Cockroach Janta Party-led protest at Jantar Mantar in New Delhi over examination irregularities.

The counsel for the petitioner mentioned the PIL before a Bench of Chief Justice D.K. Upadhyaya and Justice Tejas Karia and requested that the case be listed on Friday (July 23, 2026). “This is against the so-called student agitation. They have made the entire Delhi hostage. Roads have been blocked. Citizens of Delhi are suffering,” lawyer Barun Kumar Sinha submitted. “Alright. Tomorrow,” Chief Justice Upadhyaya said.
